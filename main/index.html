It can refer to a young male child and is used more commonly in everyday conversation. It doesn't have a specific age range, but it generally refers to boys up to their early teens. Example sentences: あの公園で遊んでいる男の子たちは楽しそうです。 The boys playing in that park look like they are having fun. 彼には二人の男の子がいます。 He has two boys. 男の子が怪我をして泣いている。 A boy is crying because he got hurt. 男の子はおもちゃの車で遊ぶのが好きです。 Boys like to play with toy cars.
